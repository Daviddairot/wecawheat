Services

The West and Central Africa Wheat Collaborative Network offers a range of comprehensive services aimed at advancing wheat productivity, production, and farmers' income using appropriate technologies, as well as fostering wheat investment across the region

Technology Adoption and Transfer

Facilitating the adoption and transfer of appropriate agricultural technologies to farmers, enabling them to leverage modern methods for enhanced wheat cultivation, harvesting, and post-harvest handling.

Read More
Training and Capacity Building

Organizing workshops, training sessions, and educational programs to build the capacity of farmers and stakeholders. This includes training in modern farming techniques, sustainable practices, and the effective use of technology

Read More
Research and Development

Conducting research and development initiatives to improve wheat varieties, pest and disease management, soil health, and climate-resilient agricultural practices.

Read More
Policy Advocacy and Support

Engaging with policymakers and advocating for policies that support the wheat industry's growth and sustainability. This includes providing policy recommendations based on research and the collective expertise of stakeholders.

Read More

About Us

The West and Central Africa Wheat Collaborative Network (WECAWheat) is a Non-Profit organization of various Wheat stakeholders from West and Central Africa, Created on March 2023 with the overarching goal of enhancing wheat productivity, production and farmer's income using appropriate technologies and to boost wheat investment in the wheat sector in West and Central Africa

Why Choose Us?

Expertise and Focus

WECAWheat is dedicated to wheat production and the improvement of farmers livelihood in West and Central Africa. Our organization's specialized focus ensures deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities specific to this region. Our initiatives are designed to have a tangible, positive impact on wheat productivity, production, and ultimately, farmers' income

Collaborative Approach

We bring together various stakeholders, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing among experts, farmers, industry players, and researchers. This collaborative approach amplifies our collective impact and allows for the implementation of comprehensive, multifaceted solutions.

Innovative Technologies

WECAWheat leverages cutting-edge, appropriate technologies tailored to the West and Central African context. By staying at the forefront of advancements, we enable farmers to optimize their production, reduce inefficiencies, and increase yields sustainably. Together, we can achieve a future of enhanced wheat productivity, increased income, and a more prosperous agricultural sector.
