About Us

The West and Central Africa Wheat Collaborative Network (WECAWheat) is a Non-Profit organization of various Wheat stakeholders from West and Central Africa, Created on March 2023 with the overarching goal of enhancing wheat productivity, production and farmer's income using appropriate technologies and to boost wheat investment in the wheat sector in West and Central Africa
