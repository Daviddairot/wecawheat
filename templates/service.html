Services

The West and Central Africa Wheat Collaborative Network offers a range of comprehensive services aimed at advancing wheat productivity, production, and farmers' income using appropriate technologies, as well as fostering wheat investment across the region

Technology Adoption and Transfer

Facilitating the adoption and transfer of appropriate agricultural technologies to farmers, enabling them to leverage modern methods for enhanced wheat cultivation, harvesting, and post-harvest handling.

Training and Capacity Building

Organizing workshops, training sessions, and educational programs to build the capacity of farmers and stakeholders. This includes training in modern farming techniques, sustainable practices, and the effective use of technology

Research and Development

Conducting research and development initiatives to improve wheat varieties, pest and disease management, soil health, and climate-resilient agricultural practices.

Policy Advocacy and Support

Engaging with policymakers and advocating for policies that support the wheat industry's growth and sustainability. This includes providing policy recommendations based on research and the collective expertise of stakeholders.
